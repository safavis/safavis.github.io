My name is Mahya Safavi and I obtained my Ph.D. from EECS department at University of California, Irvine. My PhD focus has been brain-computer interface, and wearble devices under supervison of Pai H. Chou. I had a great experience internship at Livmor Inc., where we worked on an application to detect atrial fibrillation using PPG sensor. Prior to UC Irvine, I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc in electrical engineering from University of Tehran and Sharif University of Technology Tehran, Iran.
